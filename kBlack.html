Courses Taught

With the help of this course, students can increase or enhance their knowledge and abilities in the areas of hospitality and tourism, which are represented in the numerous tourist industry sectors. In addition to learning about food preparation and presentation, facility evaluation, inventory control, marketing, and event and activity management, students will also research customer service concepts and the cultural and economic factors that influence travel patterns. The tourism industry's job options, environmental and societal challenges, and health and safety requirements will all become more apparent to the students.
